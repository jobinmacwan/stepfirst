Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

StepFirst ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

StepFirst collects the following types of information:

  • Health Data: Step count data accessed through Apple HealthKit to track your daily walking progress.
  • App Usage Data: Information about which apps you choose to block, stored locally on your device using Apple's Screen Time API.
  • User Preferences: Your step goals, settings, and achievement progress stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Track your daily step progress toward your goals
  • Manage app blocking based on your step completion
  • Display your achievements and statistics
  • Improve the app experience

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your health data, app usage information, or personal details to any external servers. Your information never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

StepFirst integrates with the following Apple services:

  • Apple HealthKit: To read your step count data
  • Apple Screen Time API (Family Controls): To manage app restrictions

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your health and usage data remains private and on your device.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from Apple's built-in device security features including encryption.

Children's Privacy

StepFirst is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data within the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions through iOS Settings
  • Revoke Screen Time permissions through iOS Settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
