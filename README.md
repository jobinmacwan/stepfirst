## How It Works

1. **Set Your Daily Step Goal** - Choose a step target that works for your lifestyle
2. **Select Apps to Block** - Pick apps like Instagram, TikTok, or games you want to limit
3. **Walk to Unlock** - Complete your step goal to automatically unlock all blocked apps
4. **Build Healthy Habits** - Track streaks, earn achievements, and reduce screen time

## Privacy

Your health data stays on your device. StepFirst uses Apple HealthKit to read step data locally and never uploads your personal information to external servers.
